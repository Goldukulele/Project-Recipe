Salmon Recipe

Description

This simple salmon recipe is not only healhy but incredibly delicious!

Prep and cooking time is less than 10 minutes and is sure to give a smile on your face :)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb Salmon with skin
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Pinch of Pepper
  • Avocado Oil or Ghee Butter (1 tbsp)
  • Slice of Lemon

Steps

  1. Add salt and pepper on both sides of the salmon.
  2. Add avocodo oil or ghee butter onto pan.
  3. Heat pan with medium-high heat.
  4. Place salmon (skin side) onto pan. Sear for 45 seconds
  5. Turnover salmon and sear other side for 45 seconds
  6. Lower heat to low-medium. Leave salmon for another 45 seconds
  7. Turnover salmon and cook for another 45 seconds
  8. Take salmon out and squeeze lemon juice on the fish
  9. Enjoy your delicious salmon!
